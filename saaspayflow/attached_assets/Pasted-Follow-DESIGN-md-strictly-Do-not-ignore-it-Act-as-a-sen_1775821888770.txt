### 1. Typography (highest priority)

Fix all typography across dashboard:

* Use sentence case everywhere

* Remove ALL CAPS (except tiny labels if absolutely needed)

* Apply strict hierarchy:

  H1 → 32px bold
  H2 → 24px semibold
  H3 → 18px medium
  Body → 14px
  Small/meta → 12px

* Reduce description text size

* Keep descriptions short (max 1 line)

* Add proper spacing between:

  * heading → description (8–12px)
  * sections → sections (24–32px)

Typography must feel:

* calm
* readable outdoors
* high contrast but not harsh

---

### 2. Content Simplification

Rewrite ALL text to be:

* short
* direct
* human
* financial-focused

❌ "Create your first invoice to unlock reminders and insights"

❌ "See what is working and where cash is leaking"
✅ "Track your cash flow"

Avoid:

* buzzwords
* long sentences
* marketing tone
